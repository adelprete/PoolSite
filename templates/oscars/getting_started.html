Oscar Office Pools: Getting Started

How it works:

Users can create and join Oscar Pools with their friends or co-workers where they will compete to see who can correctly guess the winners of each category at the Academy Awards. Members of each pool will have up to the start of the Award Ceremony to fill out or make changes to their Ballots. During the ceremony our team will be updating the scores in real time, so you'll know who in your pool is winning and losing throughout the entire ceremony!

Different Ways to Win:

The Admin of each pool will choose one of the following formats:

Win By Number of Correct Picks:
Its simple, winners in this format are decied by who correctly guesses the most award winners.

Win By Accumulated Points:
In this format the Admin of the Pool will assign points to each category. So if a Member guesses the winner of a category worth 10 pts their Ballot will gain 10pts. The Member whose ballot has the most points by the end of Award Ceremony wins.

Predictions

As users fill out and submit their ballots you'll be able to see who the community thinks will win in each category!

Thats It!

Now go create an Oscar Pool and let us do all the hard work for you! Be sure to watch the Oscars live on ABC to follow along!
