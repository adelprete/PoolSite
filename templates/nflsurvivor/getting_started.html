NFL Survivor Office Pools: Getting Started

How it works:

The each member of a pool will try to guess a single winner for each week of the NfL Season. The last member of a pool who hasn't guessed incorrectly is the winner of the pool. What makes it tricky part is that you can't guess the same team twice.

So if you choose New England Patriots to win for Week 1, you can't choose the Patriots again for any other week.

Depending on the settings of your pool, you have two ways of making your picks:

Week By Week: This is probably the most popular. Members of the pool have up until the first game of each week to pick their winner for that week. So you can make all of your picks for the whole year, and then come back each week if you wanted to change your pick. As soon as the first game starts for a week, no one can change their picks for that week.

All at Once: With this format members have a deadline to submit all of their picks for the entire year. Once the deadline passes, everyone's picks are locked and no one in the pool can edit their picks for the rest of the year. So you're submitting all your picks at the beginning of the season, and hope it all plays out like you hoped.

How to Win:

As the season goes on, members in your pool will start guessing the wrong team to win. And as soon as you guess the wrong team, you're out. So to win, you have to be the last surviving person in your pool that hasn't made a wrong prediction yet.
