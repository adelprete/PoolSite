Pool Format:

How-To-Play:

For each week of the season you will select a team that you think will win.
You cannot choose the same team twice so choose wisely for each week.
You lose once the team you chose to win loses their game that week.
The last remaining member of the pool who still hasn't had a team lose wins the pool.

What's the Selection Process

{% if pool.nflsurvivorpool.selection_process == 'weekly' %}

This Pool selected 'Week By Week'. This means that you'll have until the start of a week to get in your pick for that week. So when the first game of the week starts, and you don't have a pick for that week, your pick sheet will be eliminated. Remember, most weeks start on Thursday night.

{% else %}

This Pool selected 'All At Once'. This means that you must fill out your entire pick sheet with who you think is going to win each week of the season before your pool starts. You will not have a chance to edit your picks once this pool has started.
