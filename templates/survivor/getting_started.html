Survivor Office Pools: Getting Started

How it works:

Users can create and join Survivor Pools with their friends or co-workers. Before the season starts, each member will fill out picksheets where they will choose the 4 castaways who they think will be the best this season. No combination of 4 castaways can be the same so act quick before someone in your pool picks the 4 castaways you wanted!

How to Win:

Throughout the season your castaways will perform certain events that will net your pick sheet points. So if your pick sheet has a castaway named Tony, and Tony finds a hidden immunity idol, your pick sheet will get 4 points! (the point breakdown is below) The pool member whose pick sheet accumulated the most points by the end of the season wins!

Point Breakdown

Your pick sheet will gain points when your castaways perform the following events:

Castaway is the first person voted out - 12pts
Castaway's tribe/group wins reward - 2pts
Castaway's tribe wins immunity - 2pts
Castaway is a part of the majority vote during a tribal council - 1pts
Castaway makes the Merge - 3pts Castaway wins an individual Immunity Challenge - 3pts
Castaway wins an individual reward - 3pts
Castaway won the challenge at redemption island - 1pts
Castaway finds a hidden immunity idol - 4pts
Castaway returned from Redemption Island - 4pts
Castaway used a hidden idol to not get voted out - 6pts
Castaway gets two points for every vote at the final tribal counsel - 2pts

Thats It!

Now go create a Survivor Pool and let us do all the hard work for you! Hurry up, the season premieres on Sept 24th on CBS!
