How can I change my password?

To change your password got to our Request Password Change form and follow the onscreen steps.

How do I deactivate or delete my account?

On our contact page, fill out the form completely and tell us whether you want your account deleted or just deactivated. Your message will receive a response to shortly.

How do I reactivate my account?

On our contact page, fill out the form completely and tell us that you'd like to reactivate your account. Your account will be reactivated that same day.
