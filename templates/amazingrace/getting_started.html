Amazing Race Office Pools: Getting Started

How it works:

Users can create and join Amazing Race Pools with their friends or co-workers. Before the season starts, each member will fill out picksheets where they will choose the 3 teams who they think will be the best this season. No combination of 3 teams can be the same so act quick before someone in your pool picks the 3 teams you wanted!

How to Win:

Throughout the season your teams will perform certain events that will net your pick sheet points. So if your pick sheet has a team named Dave & Conner, and Dave & Conner gets first place on a leg, your pick sheet will get 1 point! (the point breakdown is below) The pool member whose pick sheet accumulated the most points by the end of the season wins!

Point Breakdown

Your pick sheet will gain points when your teams perform the following events:

Team is the first to complete the Detour/Roadblock - 1pt
Team used an Obstacle on another team(UTurn,Yield,etc) - 1pt
Team completes a leg - 1pt
Team are first to arrive at the pit stop - 1pt
Team was the first to be eliminated - 6pts

Thats It!
